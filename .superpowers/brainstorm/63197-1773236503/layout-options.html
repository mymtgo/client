B: Compact Sidebar, Wide Detail

Narrower list (~25%) with just name and format. More room for the decklist on the right — enough to show a two-column card layout (main + sideboard side by side). Color identity shown only in the detail panel.

Both options include:

  • Search bar to filter archetypes by name
  • Format filter pills/dropdown
  • Visual indicator for downloaded vs not-downloaded decklists
  • "Download decklist" button with progress when decklist not yet fetched
  • Staleness notice when decklist is over 1 week old
